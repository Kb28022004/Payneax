About Our Company

Empowering Retailers & Distributors

PayNeax is a trusted B2B fintech service provider offering a complete digital financial services platform for Retailers, Distributors, and Super Distributors across India. We operate on a structured B2B model, enabling our partners to deliver essential financial services to customers efficiently and securely.

Our platform is designed to support the creation and management of multi-level business networks , allowing retailers and distributors to offer Mobile & DTH Recharge, Utility Bill Payments, Money Transfer (DMT), and AEPS services through a single, easy-to-use system.

At PayNeax, our focus is on service reliability, transparency, and business growth. We provide a ready-to-use fintech ecosystem where partners can start operations quickly without the complexity of technical integrations or APIs. Our systems are monitored continuously to ensure high uptime, fast transaction processing, and accurate settlements.

We understand the needs of ground-level retailers and growing distribution networks. That’s why PayNeax emphasizes simple onboarding, real-time reporting, secure transactions, and dedicated support , helping our partners scale their businesses with confidence.
